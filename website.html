Frequently Asked Questions

When will LocalNow launch? ▼
LocalNow is launching in 2026 across major cities in Canada and the United States. We're currently signing up early access users and merchants.
Is it really free for businesses? ▼
Yes! There are zero listing fees for businesses joining LocalNow. We make money through transaction commissions when you earn sales, not through upfront costs.
Will I pay subscription fees as a customer? ▼
Never. LocalNow is completely free to download and use. You only pay for the actual services you order or book. No hidden fees or premium memberships.
How do I earn rewards? ▼
Every purchase on LocalNow earns you reward points automatically. Accumulate points to unlock discounts, free items, and exclusive deals from your favorite merchants.
How quickly can I set up as a business? ▼
Business onboarding takes just 10-15 minutes. You provide basic information, upload your menu or services, set pricing, and you're ready to receive orders.
What cities are launching first? ▼
We're launching in Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, Miami, and Austin. Additional cities will follow shortly after launch.
